    Order confirmation

    Included in our pricing is the review of your order by our technical team. If required, they will suggest any necessary changes and optimizations. Once you confirm these changes, we will prepare the final technical drawings and proceed with production. Rest assured that our team is dedicated to ensuring the accuracy and quality of your project every step of the way.

  • Order processing

    Please allocate a timeframe of 10 to 14 business days for the shipment of your order. Each glass pane is carefully packaged individually to facilitate hassle-free unloading and handling. For optimal protection, the entire order is bundled and shipped on a sturdy wooden pallet. Once the carrier picks up the order, a tracking number will be provided to you for easy tracking of your shipment.

  • @include('../separator.html',{'InnerText':'Final steps'})

    Shipping

    For the delivery of your order, a signature will be required upon receipt. In the case of smaller orders consisting of 3-4 glass panes or less, the delivery will be made directly to your doorstep. For larger orders, we provide free curbside delivery, which requires your assistance in unloading the order alongside the driver. If needed, we can also arrange tailgate delivery upon request.

    Shipping details

  • Reception and Installation

    Before proceeding with the installation, we recommend thoroughly inspecting all boxes and packages and verifying the piece count against your order confirmation. To assist you in the installation process, we provide a comprehensive step-by-step installation guide and video, ensuring a smooth completion. If you prefer personalized guidance, you can request a phone meeting with one of our technicians who can provide valuable installation tips.
