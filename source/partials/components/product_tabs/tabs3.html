Edge finishing

What is tempered glass?

For all glass thicknesses, the minimum distance from the edge of the hole to the nearest edge of the glass must be the greatest between 6 mm and twice the glass thickness.

The minimum distance from the edge of the hole to a corner of the glass must be 6.5 times the thickness of the glass when the corner is 90 degrees and more.

Minimum distance between holes

For all glass thicknesses, the minimum distance between holes is the greatest distance between: 10 mm and twice the glass thickness

Dimension of round holes

For all glass thicknesses, the minimum distance between holes is the greatest distance between: 6,4 mm and glass thickness
